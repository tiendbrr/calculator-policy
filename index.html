Privacy Policy

App:Quick Calculator

Last updated: July 8, 2025

1. User Data

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

2. Calculation Storage

Calculations performed by users are saved locally on their devices. This data is never transmitted to any server. Users can view, edit, or delete their saved calculations at any time.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any special permissions, such as access to contacts, location, camera, or microphone.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, such as advertising platforms or analytics tools, that could collect or process user data.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted externally, the risk of data exposure is extremely low. However, users are encouraged to keep their devices secure.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be communicated through app updates.
